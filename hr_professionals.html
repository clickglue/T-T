Why would you consider this program?

Your employees navigate a fast-paced world filled with both opportunities and challenges. To thrive in this environment, maintaining balance and mental strength is essential. More and more employers recognize the value of proactively supporting their teams to help them stay healthy, engaged, and motivated. Tribe & Thrive is designed for forward-thinking employers committed to fostering a thriving workplace culture. Our program equips employees with the tools to sustain their health, build resilience, and reach their full potential—ultimately reducing absences caused by burnout. For you as an employer, this means cultivating a productive, engaged workforce that feels supported and valued every step of the way. Together, we can create a positive, balanced, and thriving work environment.

What is the program?

It is a 6 day fully imersive journey, split in three convenient blocks. You can find the content of the program here

How do I identify participants?

We have developed an easy to use tool to help you identify which employees would benefit most from the Tribe & Thrive program. This tool is designed to guide you in spotting individuals who could gain the most value from the program, based on their current roles, challenges, and aspirations.

Who developed it?

Why was it developed?

Both [Company A] and [Company B] are responsible employers. Developing Tribe & Thrive is part of their responsible employer strategy.

As the architects of the Tribe & Thrive program, coaches Ilse, Anouk, and Catherine have the opportunity to share their expertise in personal sustainability and resilience, helping both employees and organizations thrive in a meaningful and lasting way. We invite you to join this growing group of responsible employers who are taking proactive steps to support their people and create a thriving work environment. Together, we can build a positive and sustainable future for all.

What is the businesscase?

Costs of long term absence are significant. The investment for the program is € 3.500 per participant.
