Help your employees and your business to prevent long term absence

Your employees live in a demanding world.
This generates the strongly increasing number of people who are long term absent from you organization.
Many employers are aware of this challenge, and are committed to support their employees in prevention.
Tribe & Thrive is a program by and for these responsible employers.
It is build to support their employees in maintain their balance and support them in their mental health.

How do I identify participants?

We have developed a simple tool you can use to identify which employees can benefit from the program.

Who developed it?

The program was initiated by a team of HR professionals form Toyota and Siemens Healthcare, together with the consultancy firm HazelHeartwood.
It was further developped by a team of specialized coaches, with hands on experience in coaching employees in their personal sustainability.

Why was it developed?

Both Toyota and Siemens Healthcare are responsible employers. Developing Tribe & Thrive is part of their responsible employer strategy.
HazelHeartwood has an objective in social innovation. We already started Experience@Work. We beleive we can help both employees and employers with this program.

What is the businesscase?

Costs of long term absence are significant, an estimated total financial costs of €18.500. The investment for the program is € 3.500 per participant.
