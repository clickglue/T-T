Who is it for?

We live in a demanding world. We want to work hard, be good parents, maintain relationships, discover new parts of the world. Often we are pushed to the boundaries of our mental and physical capabilities. Sometimes we run the risk of going over these limits, loosing our balance. Then we find ourselves in a situation where we need a long time detached from everything to regain our enthousiasm and motivation. This comes at great cost for you as individual, your environment and the employer. Nobody should take this risk.
If you this program might help you to maintain your personal sustainability, please talk to HR!

What do you learn?

The program brings you practical tools and insights that can help you to keep track of your mental condition, and that enables you to translmate this into action when that is needed. You can find the program here..

How much time does it cost you?

The program is designed as a journey with three sessions: a block of 2.5 days, then about a month later another 2 days, and then 1 return day. The evenings are also part of the program.

Where is it?

The program is organized in The Old Abbey in Kortenberg. The participants are invited to stay in a hotel nearby.
