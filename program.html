THE VISION BEHIND THE PROGRAM

Sustainable personal and professional growth doesn’t happen by chance—it requires a structured approach rooted in proven methodologies. The Tribe & Thrive program is designed using a combination of behavioral science, change management principles, and experiential learning to create lasting impact. One of the key frameworks that influenced the structure of this program is the ADKAR model, which outlines the five essential stages of successful change. · Awareness – Understanding the need for growth and sustainability. · Desire – Building motivation to actively engage in change. · Knowledge – Learning the necessary tools and strategies. · Ability – Practicing new skills and habits. · Reinforcement – Embedding these changes into daily life. Each module aligns with these principles, guiding participants through a journey of self-awareness, interpersonal development, and continuous improvement. By combining scientific insights, hands-on exercises, and peer learning, we ensure that employees not only gain valuable knowledge but also develop the resilience and confidence to apply it effectively in their work and personal lives. With this foundation in mind, let’s explore the program structure in more detail.

PROGRAM AGENDA

Module 1: Intrapersonal Awareness and Desire

Growth starts from within. This module focuses on self-awareness, personal sustainability, and proactive change. Participants will explore their motivations, understand stress and its impact, and learn how to take control of their habits for long-term success.
    Day 1: Meet Them at Their Bus Stop
  • Understanding personal sustainability
  • Clarifying motivation and making it tangible
  • Self-assessment: Where are you today?
    Day 2: Understanding and Managing Stress
  • What is stress, and how does it impact you?
  • Recognizing stressors and their effects
  • Practical strategies to manage and reduce stress
    Day 3: Proactivity and (new) Habit Formation
  • The circle of impact and influence
  • Shifting from reactive to proactive behavior
  • Implementing new habits: Getting started and staying consistent

Module 2: Interpersonal Growth

Expanding Knowledge and Abilities Strong interpersonal skills are essential for a thriving workplace. This module helps participants improve collaboration, communication, and conflict management. They will explore the foundations of effective teamwork, understand conflict dynamics, and learn strategies for setting boundaries while fostering positive relationships.
    Day 4: Effective Collaboration
  • The why, how, and what of meaningful interactions
  • Key principles of successful teamwork
  • Enhancing communication and cooperation
    Day 5: Conflict Management and Boundary Setting
  • Understanding conflict: Origins and impact
  • Avoiding vs. addressing conflict constructively
  • Setting and maintaining healthy boundaries

Module 3: Repeated Learning and Fine-Tuning

Sustainable growth requires ongoing reflection and adjustment. This final module provides a space for participants to revisit key insights, share experiences, and refine their approach based on real-life applications. Through group discussions and peer learning, they will strengthen their ability to integrate the concepts from previous modules into daily life.
    Day 6: Return Day – Deepening Insights and Sharing Experiences
  • Revisiting key topics from Modules 1 & 2
  • Group discussions based on real-life applications
  • Peer learning through intervision and networking
  • Fine-tuning strategies for sustainable personal and professional growth

PERSONALIZED START AND SUPPORT THROUGHOUT THE JOURNEY

To ensure a meaningful and impactful experience, the program begins with a one-on-one intake session. This session aligns expectations, helps participants set personal goals, and provides a foundation for their individual growth throughout the program. Additionally, each participant will have the opportunity to schedule a personal coaching session with one of our expert coaches at any point before the final return day. This allows for deeper reflection, tailored guidance, and support in overcoming personal challenges. Throughout the program, a Personal Development Plan (PDP) will be used to help participants stay focused on what matters most to them. By setting clear intentions and tracking progress, each individual can maximize their learning and ensure long-term, sustainable growth.
